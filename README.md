## Hi there 👋

I am Henok a Research Fellow in AI Systems  
University of Cape Town  

🔬 AI for Dynamical Systems | STGNNs | Physics-informed ML  

---

Researcher in AI for dynamical systems, working at the intersection of machine learning, 
numerical analysis, and nonlinear dynamics.

My work focuses on spatio-temporal graph neural networks (STGNNs), physics-informed models, 
and scientific machine learning for predictive modelling of complex systems. I study how learning 
architectures interact with nonlinear instability, with emphasis on predictability horizons, 
stability and chaotic dynamics in high-dimensional systems.
